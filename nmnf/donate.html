Fundraising Goal

Our Primary goal is to procure EMS equipped ambulance with a budget of 25,00,000 (25 lakhs). We will also be procuring Portable EMS systems to put in Place in Helicopters and Fixed Wing aircraft to facilitate transfers of critical care patients from regional hospitals to and from Kathmandu. Data shows many patients are referred to or brought directly to Kathmandu from other regional hospitals for the best possible care. Medical camps in remote areas are frequently necessary with better doctors as many do not even contemplate a trip to Kathmandu. For example, many ethnic Bhote elders, male and female in remote Sankhuwasabha villages of Hungung, Chhepuwa, Hatia are dying of Cancer triggered by Gastritis at young age. This is just one example of what may be an epidemic in many other remote areas where way of life is primitive and people are choicelessly working hard. They are also deprived of fresh fruits and vegetables that the urban folks in the mid hills and southern cities get to enjoy. Additionally, we will be raising funds to purchase and stock Equipment and Gears essential for Search and Rescue Situation. This goal we believe will be achieved with your generous donations. We certainly would like to break the trend of receiving donations from foreign citizens initially and demonstrate that this is purely an initiation of our Nepali brothers and sisters from all walk of life. In future, any support extended to us will be welcomed and we will put in place a system to sustain this project perpetually. Our training and development program will highlight such issues and raise awareness.

The heritage sites of Nepal, a treasure of architectural marvels were built by our ancestor collective and selfless efforts to give back to the community and it proudly gives us the grandeur to enjoy.
