What We Do ?

Our Area Of Expertise Includes

Medics/ Paramedics

NMMF will have several Medics who will accompany pilots and Rescue Specialists with lifesaving medical kits during rescue intervention enabling quick medical attention to Ill and injured Nepalese citizens as well visiting Trekkers and Climbers in the Mountains. These Medics are familiar of various internationally acknowledged systems and equipment.

Moutain Guides

The Core team consists of several trained International Mountain Guides who are familiar with the terrains as well as adopt well with the harsh conditions in technically challenging Mountainous areas. The guides are trained in basic medications/ first aid to attend the injured individuals and are equipped with tools to conduct search and rescue. Our guides are familiar with the systems widely used in the European alp.

Aviations

With the pool of pilots trained in Switzerland to conduct Search and Rescue, our flying crew team is carefully chosen. Rescue interventions are carefully and thoroughly planned and risks are mitigated ensuring safe operations.


Under this we run various kinds of programs such as:

  1. Search and Rescue, Medical Evacuation, Body Recovery
  2. Training & Development
    • Local Level – Basic First Aid and Immobilization of Victims
    • Professional Level – Dispatching a qualified Mountain Guide and Doctors with
    • Paramedic treatment
