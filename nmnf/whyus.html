Sense of Responsibility



During Major Catastrophes, it is evident that a team of trained and qualified rescue personnel should be on standby in Nepal to quickly respond the situation. A call and wait situation for our citizens and visiting foreigners will cost lives by minutes during major disaster and we need to have trained manpower ready to be dispatched to save lives. In Nepal, The capacity of people and will is without a question available but the specific training in handling them is the missing part of the pie. Due to challenging altitude, it is in the best interest of people to recruit people who can well adapt in the high altitudes and all rescue should accompany a medic.
